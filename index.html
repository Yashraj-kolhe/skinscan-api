🛡️ Prevention & Care
Evidence-based skin health guidelines
☀️
UV Protection
Shield your skin from harmful sun radiation
Apply SPF 30+ sunscreen 20 mins before going outdoors
Reapply every 2 hours, especially after swimming or sweating
Wear wide-brim hats and UV-blocking sunglasses
Seek shade between 10 AM – 4 PM (peak UV hours)
UV rays penetrate clouds — protect skin on overcast days too
⚡ UV Index above 6? Stay in shade or cover up.

💧
Skin Hydration & Diet
Nourish your skin from within
Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily to maintain skin elasticity
Eat antioxidant-rich foods — berries, leafy greens, tomatoes
Use a fragrance-free moisturiser twice daily
Avoid smoking — it accelerates skin ageing and cell damage
Omega-3 fatty acids (fish, flaxseed) reduce inflammation
🥦 A balanced diet is your skin's best long-term defence.
